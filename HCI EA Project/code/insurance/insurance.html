Overview of Car Insurance

Car insurance provides financial protection against physical damage or bodily injury resulting from traffic collisions. It also offers financial protection against theft of the vehicle and damage to the vehicle sustained from events other than traffic collisions.

Benefits of Car Insurance

  • Financial coverage for accidents
  • Protection against theft
  • Liability coverage for injuries
  • Peace of mind while driving
