About Legend Motor Limited

Legend Motor Limited is excited to announce the launch of two new service lines specifically designed to enhance your experience in purchasing vehicles and insurance within Hong Kong. Building upon the System Development Project undertaken in Semesters Two and Three, we are committed to delivering exceptional value and service to our customers.

Our Mission

At Legend Motor Limited, our mission is to provide a delightful sales experience while ensuring that our customers find the best vehicles and insurance plans suited to their needs. We believe in transparency, integrity, and a customer-first approach in everything we do.

With a wide array of partnering dealers, we offer a diverse selection of vehicles to cater to various preferences and budgets. Our new insurance service line provides comprehensive options to protect your investment, ensuring peace of mind while you enjoy your new vehicle.
